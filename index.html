Q&A

What services do you offer? +

I offer a full range of copywriting and marketing services including email marketing, sales copy, website copy, ad copy, content marketing, and complete marketing strategy development. Each service is tailored to your specific business needs and goals.

How long does a project take? +

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple email sequence might take 1-2 weeks, while a complete marketing funnel could take 4-6 weeks. I'll provide a clear timeline during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

What are your rates? +

My rates depend on the project scope, deliverables, and timeline. I offer both project-based pricing and monthly retainer options. Let's schedule a call to discuss your needs, and I'll provide a custom quote that fits your budget and goals.

Do you work with businesses in my industry? +

I've worked with clients across various industries including SaaS, e-commerce, coaching, consulting, fitness, finance, and more. The principles of great copywriting and marketing strategy apply universally. I take time to understand your specific market and audience to create tailored solutions.

What results can I expect? +

While results vary by industry and current situation, my clients typically see improved conversion rates, increased engagement, higher ROI on ad spend, and better customer retention. I focus on measurable outcomes and provide regular reports to track progress and optimize campaigns.
